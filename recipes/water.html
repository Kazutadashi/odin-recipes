Water

Description:

This is how to make a refreshing glass of water. This recipe is good year round, but especially during the summer months.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup
  • 6 cubes of ice
  • 1 cup of purified water

Steps:

  1. Take cup and shave 1 ice cub into cup
  2. Add 1/4 cup of water to the cup, and mix with the ice shavigs
  3. Stir until ice shavings are dissolved into the water
  4. Add rest of the water to cup
  5. Finally, add the ice cubes
