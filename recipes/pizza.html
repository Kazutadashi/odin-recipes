Pizza

Description

This is a PIZZA!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Phone
  • Around 12 dollars
  • 1 Credit card
  • 1 device capable of internet access

Steps:

  1. Use phone and call local pizza place
  2. Ask if they do delivery
  3. Order the pizza
  4. If they do, pay over the phone or visit their website
  5. Wait for pizza to arrive at residence
