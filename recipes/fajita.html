Fajita

Description

This fantastically fun fajita idea will make everyone go crazy at your parties. It is not only tasty, but very healthy too with the plentiful proportions of vegetables and protein.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb. Chicken
  • 1 Bell peppers of your choice
  • 1/2 Onion
  • Pinch of Salt and Pepper
  • Cilantro to taste
  • Flour or Corn Tortillas

Steps:

  1. Salt and pepper the raw chicken front and back.
  2. Place chicken on skillet or pan
  3. Set heat on stove to high
  4. Cook chicken until internal temperature is safe
  5. Take chicken out of pan, and cook vegetables until the onions are golden brown
  6. Place on tortilla and add cilantro to taste.
  7. Enjoy!
